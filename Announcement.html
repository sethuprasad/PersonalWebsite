MRTL Announcement

MRTL seeks accomplished scientists and highly motivated students to join our team. Below are available job opportunities for currently funded projects and new projects in development.

Open Positions:

  • Postdoctoral Researcher - Conduct independent and collaborative research in areas such as Microelectronics Metrology, Secure Edge Computing/IoT, Artificial Intelligence Hardware & Software, and Quantum Technology. Requires a PhD in a relevant field. Experience with field work, Microelectronics Metrology and advanced Semiconductor Technology preferred.
  • PhD Student (1 - 2 positions) - Passionate about advancing metrology research and applications to address measurement challenges related to microelectronics manufacturing and to innovate in semiconductors. The research will focus on advances and breakthroughs in measurement science, standards, material characterization, instrumentation, testing, and manufacturing capabilities that will accelerate the underlying research and development for metrology of next-generation microelectronics. Example projects include innovations in measurements that are accurate, precise, and fit for the production of microelectronic materials, devices, circuits, and systems.
  • Graduate Research Assistants (2 positions) - Assist with field sampling, laboratory analyses, and data analysis on research projects related to Microelectronic Metrology research and training. Requires enrollment in a graduate program in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Informatics, Data Science, Cybersecurity, or Information Technology. Previous research experience preferred.
  • Student Workers (3 positions) - Support field and/or lab components of research projects. Duties may include sample processing, data entry, equipment maintenance, and general lab assistance. Requires enrollment as an undergraduate student. Previous lab experience helpful but not required.

To apply for any of these positions or to obtain more information about open positions in our lab, please email "MRTL@nau.edu" with a cover letter, CV or resume, and contact information for three references. Review of applications will begin immediately and continue until positions are filled.
