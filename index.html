MERP - Metrology Education & Research Project

Aligned with national CHIPS Act priorities, MERP bolsters the nation’s microelectronics industry in two key areas: workforce development and research. To cultivate semiconductor talent, we propose launching a Microelectronics Metrology Certificate program which will provide critical workforce training and help meet semiconductor labor demands. Additionally, to advance microelectronics innovation, we propose establishing an interdisciplinary, campus-wide Microelectronics Metrology Center focused on metrology research and development for semiconductor manufacturing. By bringing together NAU expertise across disciplines, this R&D hub will generate impactful solutions while fostering collaboration with industry partners. Together, these workforce and research efforts reinforce NAU's commitment to strengthening the nation’s microelectronics leadership.
